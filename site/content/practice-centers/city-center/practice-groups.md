title: "Practice Groups"

---
City Center hosts a variety of ongoing drop-in study, discussion, and practice groups that meet regularly and welcome new participants. See the groups listed below for details about what is offered. And see the web [**Calendar**](/calendar); choose Event Type "Practice Groups." Also see the [**Online Zendo**](/online-programs/online-zendo) page for discussion groups.

**Click on the group you are interested in for more information and links.**

### [Meditation in Recovery](/meditation-in-recovery)

Mondays, 7:30 – 9 pm PT, In-Person AND on Zoom.  
Fridays, 7:30 – 9 pm PT, on Zoom.

This link provides Meditation in Recovery Program info for City Center, Hartford Street Zen Center, and other related meetings and events.

Also see [Calendar](https://www.sfzc.org/calendar); choose Event Type "Practice Groups".

### [Young Urban Zen](/practice-centers/city-center/zen-meditation-practice-city-center/practice-groups-city-center/young-urban-zen)

Tuesday evenings, 7:15 – 8:45 pm; see [Calendar](/calendar); choose Event Type "Practice Groups".  
Meets in the Buddha Hall, 300 Page Street.

### [Trans Sangha](/locations/city-center/zen-meditation-practice-city-center/practice-groups-city-center/trans-sangha)

Thursday evenings from 7:00 – 8:30 pm; see [Calendar](/calendar) for specific weekly offerings; choose Event Type "Practice Groups".  
Meets in the Zendo, 300 Page Street.

### [Saturday Sangha](/practice-centers/city-center/zen-meditation-practice-city-center/saturday-sangha)

Saturdays, 8:50 am – 12:30 pm and weekly times based on the participation of group members; see [Calendar](/calendar); choose Event Type "Practice Groups".

Individuals in the group have the opportunity to learn a variety of doanryo jobs, to study together, and to develop and deepen practice by joining with a peer group of like-minded friends.

### [Queer Dharma](/practice-centers/city-center/zen-meditation-practice-city-center/practice-groups-city-center/queer-dharma)

Saturdays, once a month, Online, 1 – 2:15 pm PT; see [Calendar](/calendar); choose Event Type "Practice Groups".  
The LGBTIQ Queer Dharma Group is an open group, and newcomers are welcome.

### [Thursday Drop-in Practice Session](https://www.sfzc.org/thursday-online-practice-session)

Thursday, once a week, Online, 5:30 – 6:30 pm PT; see [Calendar](/calendar); choose Event Type "Practice Groups".  
Join SFZC teachers for a welcoming weekly online session with meditation, Dharma reflection, and group discussion.

### [Monthly BIPOC Affinity Group](https://www.sfzc.org/locations/city-center/zen-meditation-practice-city-center/practice-groups-city-center/monthly-bipoc)

This monthly affinity group offers a space of connection and support for those who identify as Black, Indigenous, or People of Color. All are welcome. See [Calendar](/calendar); choose Event Type "Practice Groups".
