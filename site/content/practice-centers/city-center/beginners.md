### Everyone is welcome to practice at San Francisco Zen Center! Here are some ways to get started:

### **Zazen Meditation Instruction**

Come to in-person zazen (meditation) Instruction, offered from 8:40–9:40 am. You will receive posture suggestions, have questions answered, and sit with a group of beginning practitioners. Please see the [Calendar page](/calendar) or the [Sitting Meditation and Sesshins page](/practice-centers/city-center/zen-meditation-practice-city-center/sitting-meditation-sesshins-at-city-center) for upcoming dates.

You can also access the SFZC [archive of recorded instruction sessions](https://app.sfzc.org/p/qpZQYYjF). It is suggested that you watch several sessions as each one offers a unique approach.

### **Saturday Morning Meditation**

Following zazen instruction, stay for the public program which includes a period of zazen at 9:25 am, followed by a Dharma talk, Q&A with the speaker, and social time with tea and cookies. Zazen at 9:25 am, the Dharma talk, and Q&A are also offered on Saturdays in the [Online Zendo](https://www.sfzc.org/online-programs/online-zendo).

### **Introduction to Zendo Forms**

This introduction is offered on the same days as in-person zazen instruction, following the Dharma talk and Q&A, from 11:40 am–12:15 pm. Please see the [Calendar page](https://www.sfzc.org/calendar) or the [Sitting Meditation and Sesshins page](https://www.sfzc.org/practice-centers/city-center/zen-meditation-practice-city-center/sitting-meditation-sesshins-at-city-center) for upcoming dates.

### **Sunday Beginners' Sittings**

After attending zazen instruction or viewing the archived instructions, come to a monthly [Sunday Beginners’ Sitting](/practice-centers/city-center/zen-meditation-practice-city-center/beginners-city-center/sunday-beginners-sittings), which includes additional instructions in sitting and walking meditation, 20-minute meditation periods with a group of beginners, a short lecture with Q&A, and an opportunity for individual instruction.

### **Online Offerings**

If you cannot attend in person at this time, SFZC also offers programs for beginners through the [Online Zendo](https://www.sfzc.org/online-programs/online-zendo) and [SFZC Online](https://www.sfzc.org/online-page) pages, including:

[Thursday Drop-in Online Practice Session with SFZC Teachers](https://www.sfzc.org/thursday-online-practice-session) every Thursday, 5:30 – 6:30 pm PT.

### **Volunteering**

Volunteering is an excellent way to become familiar with City Center, find community, and be of service. See our [Outreach & Volunteering page](https://www.sfzc.org/giving/outreach-volunteering) for more information. Expertise not required; beginner's minds always welcomed.
