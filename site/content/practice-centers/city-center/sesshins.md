Upcoming dates for zazenkai, one-day sittings, sesshins, and zazen/forms instruction are listed at the bottom of this page.

### **Sittings**

**Beginner's Sitting**  
These sittings are for new students and those who wish to participate in a gentler schedule. They include sitting and walking meditation, lecture, discussion, and an opportunity for individual instruction. Recommended before doing a one-day sitting. [Upcoming dates](https://www.sfzc.org/sunday-beginners-sittings).

**Half-day Sittings (Zazenkai)**  
These sittings are suitable for both beginners and experienced sitters. They include scheduled periods of sitting and walking, as well as instruction in posture, breath, and the mental disposition of experiencing whatever arises.

**One-Day Sittings**  
One-day sittings offer traditional Soto Zen all-day meditation practice. Please review a typical [one-day sitting schedule](https://www.sfzc.org/files/One-day_Sitting_Schedule).

### **Sesshins**

Sesshin means to collect the heart/mind. Participants spend 3, 5, or 7 days meditating, eating, and working in silence, devoting every aspect of the day to the realization of the Buddha Way. Current members of SFZC receive a discount for sesshins. To learn more about becoming a member, visit this [link](https://giving.sfzc.org/campaign/sfzc-membership/c93583).
