### Location

City Center's Conference Center is located in a beautiful Victorian building at **308 Page Street** (next door to the main temple at the corner of Page and Laguna). Hayes Valley and the Lower Haight neighborhoods, which include a variety of shops, restaurants, and cafes, are a five- to ten-minute walk away.

### Conference Rooms

The sunny front room (33' x 13') with its high ceilings, tall windows, and hardwood floors is perfect for presentations, classes, gatherings and meetings. We can accommodate up to 30 people classroom style or 20 in a circle. It can also accommodate a yoga or movement class with up to 15 participants. A conference table, chairs, rugs, yoga mats and props, backjack floor seats and meditation cushions are also available for this room. Additionally a large monitor with hook ups for laptop, whiteboard on wheels with pens or flip charts for presentations are all available.    
  
The smaller, more casual sun room (21' x 17') with its comfortable couches and chairs, is perfect for more intimate gatherings and workshops. The rooms are connected by a kitchen area for easy access to refreshments.

### Cancellation Policy

Guests may cancel their Booking until 7 days before the event start time and will receive a full refund (including all fees) of their Booking Price. Guests may cancel their Booking between 7 days and 24 hours before the event start time and receive a 50% refund (excluding Fees) of their Booking Price. Booking cancellations submitted less than 24 hours before the event start time are not refundable.

### Location and Directions

Civic Center BART station is a 15-minute walk away. Several buses stop within a block of Zen Center, and parking is available in nearby parking garages.

From 101 North, heading towards San Francisco:

* Take Highway 101 until it ends at Octavia Blvd. and Market St.
* Continue straight across Market St. onto Octavia Blvd.
* Take a left at Fell St.
* Take the first left onto Laguna St.
* Take a right onto Page St.
* 308 Page St. will be the second building on your right.

A map and specialized directions to and from the Conference Center may be obtained from [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/place/308+Page+St,+San+Francisco,+CA+94102/@37.7738349,-122.4284969,17z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m5!3m4!1s0x808580a19986ac59:0x132498ffd15aaf8d!8m2!3d37.7738349!4d-122.4263082?hl=en).
