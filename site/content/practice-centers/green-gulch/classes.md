Classes are currently not being offered due to the ongoing concerns regarding public Health and Safety. They are usually offered on Sunday morning and Monday and Tuesday evenings. Class series start at different times of the year and are organized around six core topics:

* Buddha's Life and Early Texts
* Buddhist Psychology
* Buddhist Wisdom
* Ethics and Compassion
* Zen
* Contemporary Buddhism

Please contact stephanie.blank@sfzc.org regarding the [robe sewing class](/practice-centers/green-gulch-farm/zen-meditation-practice-green-gulch/classes-courses-green-gulch/sewing-buddha's-robe).
