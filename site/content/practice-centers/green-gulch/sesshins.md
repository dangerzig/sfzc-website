### **Sittings**

**Saturday Half-Day Introductory Sittings**  
A gentle introduction for beginning sitters. The morning includes instruction in sitting meditation (zazen) and walking meditation (kinhin), followed by alternating brief periods of zazen (20–25 minutes) and kinhin (10–15 minutes), and discussion with the leader.

**All-Day Sittings with Zoketsu Norman Fischer and** [**Everyday Zen**](http://www.everydayzen.org/)  
These traditional yet relaxed all-day sittings begin at 9 am and include meditation, a silent lunch, Dharma talk/discussion, and dokusan (private meeting with Norman or other teachers).

**One-Day Sittings**  
One-day sittings at Green Gulch Farm offer traditional Soto Zen all-day meditation practice. Formal oryoki meals are served in the zendo. Please review a typical [one-day sitting schedule](https://www.sfzc.org/files/One-day_Sitting_Schedule_sample_ggf) as well as our guidelines for [zendo conduct](https://www.sfzc.org/files/ZendoConduct_GuestInfo_ggf).

### **Sesshins**

Sesshin, meaning "to gather, collect, and convey the mind-heart," is a traditional Soto Zen meditation retreat that includes formal zendo meals, Dharma talks, sitting and walking meditation, and private interviews with senior teachers.Please review a typical sesshin schedule, as well as our guidelines for zendo conduct.
