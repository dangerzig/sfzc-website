### Hours

The bookstore is open Sundays 11:30 am – 12:30 pm. Please note that hours vary during retreat times. Please email ggfoffice@sfzc.org to confirm our open hours before visiting.

San Francisco Zen Center's [**Online Store**](https://store.sfzc.org/ ) is available for orders of books, incense, meditation supplies, and more.

To register for an event at Green Gulch Farm, use the Register Online button on the event page or call 415-865-1899.

Call the Welcome Center at 415-383-3134 regarding:

* Inquiries about reduced fees for events and San Francisco Zen Center resident student and staff discounts
* Questions about practicing at Green Gulch Farm
