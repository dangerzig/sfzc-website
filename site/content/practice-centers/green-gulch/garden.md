The garden at Green Gulch offers an opportunity to bring our meditation and work practice into relationship with the lively world of plants.

### Nursery

The Green Gulch nursery, certified by California Organic Farmers (CCOF), is open every day, year round, 9 am – 3 pm, and carries organically grown plants which flourish in our coastal climate: flowering perennials, natives, culinary and medicinal herbs, shrubs, bamboo, and fruit plants. Plants are for sale throughout the day.

### Volunteers

Volunteers are welcome to work in the garden on Thursdays, 9 am – noon. You are invited to stay for lunch. Please contact [garden@sfzc.org](mailto:garden@sfzc.org) to make arrangements.
