title: "Green Gulch at the Farmers’ Market"

---
### May – November

Green Gulch Farm organic produce is available seasonally, in San Francisco and Mill Valley.  
  
The Green Gulch stand is in the Ferry Plaza Farmers’ Market on Saturdays, 8 am – 1 pm, throughout the season until mid-October. Check out the CUESA website for more information about the market at [www.cuesa.org](http://www.cuesa.org/). ***Location:***On the street side, to the right of the clock tower facing the Ferry Building, closest to the arcade.  
  
Green Gulch Farm produce is also available at the [Mill Valley Farmers’ Market at Blithedale Shopping Center](http://www.localharvest.org/mill-valley-farmers-market-M30247) on Fridays, 9 am – 2 pm, May through October.  
  
Farm produce, fresh-baked bread, and garden delicacies are available at Green Gulch Farm outside the zendo on Sundays following the morning lecture, from around 11 am – noon, April through November.
