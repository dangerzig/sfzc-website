### Zazen Meditation Instruction

Onsite Zazen (Meditation) Instruction is offered by senior practitioners on Sundays before the Dharma talk. Please meet at 8:15 am on the North side of the Meditation hall outside. For online introduction to zazen meditation, see our [recorded instruction sessions](https://app.sfzc.org). Click on 'Practice Instruction' in the menu.

### Sunday Morning Meditation

On Sundays at 9:25 am, a period of silent meditation in the zendo is followed immediately by a Dharma talk (see [Sunday Program](/practice-centers/green-gulch-farm/zen-meditation-practice-green-gulch/sunday-program)). All are welcome to attend.

### Saturday Half-Day Introductory Sittings

This sitting is for new students and those who wish to participate in a gentler schedule. [Read more/see dates](/practice-centers/green-gulch-farm/zen-meditation-practice-green-gulch/sitting-meditation-sesshins).

### One Day Sittings

[One-day sittings](/practice-centers/green-gulch-farm/zen-meditation-practice-green-gulch/sitting-meditation-sesshins) at Green Gulch offer traditional Soto Zen all-day meditation practice. Formal oryoki meals are served in the zendo. Please be in the zendo by 4:50 am. Participants are welcome to stay for an informal dinner at 6 pm. It is recommended to have received or followed a recording of [meditation instruction](https://app.sfzc.org/p/qpZQYYjF) and participated in a [Half-Day Introductory Sitting](/practice-centers/green-gulch-farm/zen-meditation-practice-green-gulch/sitting-meditation-sesshins).

### Guest Student Stay

Guest Students reside in student housing and participate in the full meditation work schedule and attend classes, lectures, and ceremonies. This program offers short-term residency to those interested in beginning or deepening Zen practice with the support of a community. [Read more.](/practice-centers/green-gulch-farm/visits-stays-green-gulch/guest-student-stays-green-gulch)

### Volunteer Program

We invite you to work with us by becoming a volunteer at Green Gulch Farm. Volunteering is an opportunity to experience working wholeheartedly in a practice setting while giving your time and energy to the sangha. Please [read more here](/locations/green-gulch-farm/about-green-gulch/volunteer-programs-green-gulch).
