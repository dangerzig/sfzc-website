San Francisco Zen Center welcomes international visitors to participate in the International Summer Volunteer Program at Tassajara Zen Mountain Center. This program offers a unique opportunity for international participants to immerse themselves in the daily life of Tassajara, engage with the Zen community, and support the monastery’s seasonal operations. Volunteer stays are for a minimum of 2 weeks up to 4 weeks maximum. If you wish to apply, please fill out an [application form](https://www.tfaforms.com/5171940).

## About the Program

The International Summer Volunteer Program at Tassajara is an opportunity for international visitors to immerse themselves in Zen practice and community life while offering service in a structured, supportive environment. Volunteers engage in mindful participation in daily activities that sustain the monastery, deepening their connection to Zen traditions and practice.

As a volunteer, you will have the opportunity to:

* Participate in meditation and Zen practice alongside the community
* Engage in communal living and shared responsibilities in a spirit of generosity and service
* Support the monastery through volunteer activities that align with Tassajara’s spiritual and environmental mission
* Enjoy talks by Zen teachers, take workshops on Zen and Buddhism, and interact with visiting guests and Zen practitioners from around the world
* Enjoy access to Japanese-style hot springs, an extensive Buddhist library, yoga space, and vast hiking trails in the Ventana wilderness
