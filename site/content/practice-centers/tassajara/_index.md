Ways to visit Tassajara Zen Mountain Center

[Summer Practice

An opportunity for beginning and experienced Zen students to live, work, and practice at Tassajara Zen Mountain Center from April–September at no cost.](https://bit.ly/summer-at-tassajara)

[Guest Season Retreats

During the summer season (April–September), Tassajara offers a variety of retreats for guests.](/guest-season-reservations)

[Practice Periods

Practice periods offer a rare opportunity for experienced practitioners to immerse themselves in the intimacy and rigor of monastic practice at Tassajara Zen Mountain Center.](https://www.sfzc.org/locations/tassajara/zen-practice-programs/practice-periods-tassajara)

[Work Periods

Volunteer for one of Tassajara’s work periods (in April and September) to help make necessary improvements to structures and tend to the grounds.](/locations/tassajara/zen-practice-programs/work-stay/work-periods)

[About Tassajara

Explore the history of Tassajara Hot Springs, the land and its indigenous people, and the monastery.](/practice-centers/tassajara/about-tassajara)

[Contact Tassajara

Tassajara Zen Mountain Center contact information and directions.](/practice-centers/tassajara/contact-tassajara)

[International Summer Volunteers at Tassajara

This program offers an opportunity for international participants to immerse themselves in the daily life of Tassajara, engage with the Zen community, and offer service.](https://www.sfzc.org/locations/tassajara/international-summer-volunteers-tassajara)
