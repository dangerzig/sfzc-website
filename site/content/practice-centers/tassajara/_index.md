Tassajara Zen Mountain Center is the oldest Soto Zen monastery in the Western hemisphere. Located deep in the Ventana Wilderness of the Los Padres National Forest, it offers year-round monastic training and a summer guest season when visitors can enjoy the natural hot springs, mountain trails, and vegetarian cuisine while experiencing the rhythms of Zen monastic life.
