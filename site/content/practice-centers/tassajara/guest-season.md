Every year, a Gate Opening Ceremony at the end of April marks the beginning of the Tassajara summer season. From that day on, for four months, the community of Zen students at Tassajara Zen Mountain Center welcomes guests to its beautiful valley. Tassajara encourages visitors to take a few days to step away from the busyness and pressures of daily life, and find a slower rhythm of breath and body in the contemplative spirit of the valley.

While at Tassajara, guests are welcome to enjoy the hot springs, cool off in the creek, meander along the trails, float in the swimming pool, savor Tassajara’s vegetarian cuisine, and browse books and souvenirs at the Stone Office.

**Tassajara Retreats & Sangha Weeks**

1. A variety of retreats are offered at Tassajara in the summer season. Many retreats are inspired by the connection between Zen practice, the arts, and the body. These retreats include offerings on topics such as Zen and Poetry, Yoga, Leadership, and more. Zen Practice Retreats, led by experienced Zen teachers, focus on the exploration and embodiment of Dharma teachings, and may require a stronger commitment to formal practice.
2. Sangha Weeks are an opportunity for guests to immerse themselves in the daily rhythms of the resident students which includes zazen, work practice, and classes, under the guidance of a Dharma teacher.   
     
   Please see the [Tassajara Retreat Calendar](https://www.sfzc.org/locations/tassajara/guest-season-reservations/tassajara-retreat-calendar-reservations) to view all upcoming retreats and to learn more.

Reservations for the 2026 guest season will become available in mid-January 2026.

We look forward to seeing you in the valley!
