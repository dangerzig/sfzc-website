---
title: "Non-Profit Status"

---
San Francisco Zen Center is a 501(c)3 not-for-profit religious organization. Your contribution may be tax-deductible, provided no goods or services were received in return. Please consult your tax advisor for details specific to your situation.

Purchases made from San Francisco Zen Center—for example, items from the bookstore or online store, registration for a class, or overnight accommodations—are not tax-deductible.

All proceeds from purchases through San Francisco Zen Center are used to [support our mission](https://www.sfzc.org/about/mission-and-vision), and do not benefit any private individuals.
