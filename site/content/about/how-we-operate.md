As a non-profit, San Francisco Zen Center is governed through bylaws and a board of directors, and has established various policies including ethical guidelines, green practice, and an inclusion policy.

Responding to and addressing issues, conflicts, and other difficulties that may arise in the San Francisco Zen Center community.

### [Diversity, equity, inclusion, and accessibility (DEIA)](/about/how-san-francisco-zen-center-operates/diversity-equity-inclusion-and-accessibility-deia)

Diversity, equity, inclusion, and accessibility (DEIA) are embodied throughout San Francisco Zen Center's teachings, practice, organizational culture, policies, and operations as a manifestation of our vow to awaken together with all beings.
