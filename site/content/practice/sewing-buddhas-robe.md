This class is to assist those who have received permission from their teacher to sew a rakusu or okesa in preparation for receiving precepts and a robe in Jukai or Shukke Tokudo. People who have already sewn a rakusu or okesa and are interested in learning to assist as sewing teachers are welcome to come and help.

Please contact Tim Wicks or Steph Blank with any sewing inquiries:  
stephanie.blank@sfzc.org  
timothywicks@comcast.net

Classes are meeting at City Center on Thursdays from 7 – 8:30 pm.  
Before attending, please review the [Health and Safety Requirements](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1KXpatYf_6Dv-kPhXgAU6UBo9flS3Yw1SS05CbA4aWH4/edit). To find out more and receive permission to attend, please contact Tim Wicks at timothywicks@comcast.net.

**Rakusu Sewing Instructions** are available [here](/offerings/deepening-practice/instructions-sewing-rakusu). ***After receiving the sewing teacher's approval,*** a rakusu sewing kit can be purchased for $75, plus postage; click on this button:  
[Rakusu Sewing Kit](https://giving.sfzc.org/event/sewing-buddhas-robe-fees/e524019)

**To sew a rakusu for non-SFZC residents:** The registration fee for participation in the ongoing sewing classes at City Center or at Green Gulch Farm is $275. This includes materials. The cost of a prepared rakusu sewing kit only is $75 (see above). ***After receiving the sewing teacher's permission to attend classes at City Center*** (GGF has a different system for payment), pay the $275 fee by clicking on this button:  
[Rakusu Sewing Registration Fee](https://giving.sfzc.org/event/sewing-buddhas-robe-fees/e524019)

**To sew an okesa for non-SFZC residents:** The registration fee for a Head Sewing Teacher's assistance with cutting and sewing an okesa at City Center or at Green Gulch Farm is $1,200. This includes materials. ***After receiving the sewing teacher's permission to attend classes at City Center*** (GGF has a different system for payment), pay the $1,200 fee by clicking on this button:  
[Okesa Sewing Registration Fee](https://giving.sfzc.org/event/sewing-buddhas-robe-fees/e524019)

If students have difficulty meetings these costs, please discuss a sliding scale arrangement with your sewing teacher.

*Note: There is usually an additional donation made to the temple for the ceremony.*
