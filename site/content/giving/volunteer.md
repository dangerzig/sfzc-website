SFZC’s volunteers are a large community of more than 900 supporters and practitioners whose service is vital to the on-going operation of our temples. Our volunteers donate thousands of hours every year to the organization and we are grateful for their hard work and sincerely appreciate the valuable time they spend with us. Volunteering is a beautiful example of offering time and talent as a gift to SFZC.

Volunteers work alongside a wide range of people (abbots, monks, teachers, students, and staff), allowing for a deep engagement in community and fostering the sense of 'work as zazen.'

Opportunities to volunteer are available at both City Center and Green Gulch Farm. Some of the areas where we often need help are:

* Neighborhood outreach
* Gardening
* Kitchen
* Library
* Bookstore
* Special Events
* Farm Work
* Skilled Maintenance Work
* Online support

Please fill out the form below to volunteer and you will be added to the monthly Volunteer newsletter. After receiving your application, we will put you in touch with the right person to help schedule your first volunteer shift, provide additional info to help you learn more about SFZC volunteer opportunities, and confirm that volunteering is right for you. For a complete list of current volunteer opportunities [**see this document**](/file/volunteer-opportunities). If you have any questions you can reach out to [outreach@sfzc.org](mailto:outreach@sfzc.org).
