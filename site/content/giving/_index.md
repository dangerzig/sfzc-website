Your generosity sustains San Francisco Zen Center's three practice centers and growing online community. There are many ways to support our mission of making Zen practice accessible to all.

- **[Donate Now](https://giving.sfzc.org/give/170840#!/donation/checkout)**: Your support is crucial in sustaining our practice centers and online community.
- **[Become a Member](https://giving.sfzc.org/campaign/sfzc-membership/c93583)**: Deepen your connection with exclusive events and discounts.
- **[Leave a Legacy](/giving/legacy/)**: Join the Dharma Lamp Circle with a bequest or gift of any size.
- **[Volunteer](/giving/volunteer/)**: Bring your practice out of the meditation hall and into the world.

SFZC is a non-profit 501(c)(3) organization. Tax ID: 94-3143976.

For questions about giving, please contact Nannette Mickle, Director of Development, at [development@sfzc.org](mailto:development@sfzc.org).
