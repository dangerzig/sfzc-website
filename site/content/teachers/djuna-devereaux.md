Djuna Devereaux weaves asana training, with somatic movement, biomechanics, and the wisdom of yoga and the Buddha Dharma, into each offering. Her sessions are crafted to cultivate embodied awareness, as well as strength and mobility, while supporting the body’s innate capacity to heal. As a long-time Dharma practitioner, she approaches the asanas as vehicles of insight, inviting us to awaken to present moment experience through the intelligence of the body.

Djuna teaches at Spirit Rock and leads their Dharma and Yoga Teacher Trainings. She is faculty for Prajna Yoga and offers workshops and teacher trainings internationally, along with annual retreats at Green Gulch Farm and Tassajara. She is also a certified yoga therapist and Somatic Experiencing Practitioner in private practice. Djuna serves on the Sacred Mountain Sangha Leadership Council supporting her primary Dharma teachers, Thanissara and Kittisaro.
