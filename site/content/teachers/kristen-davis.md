Kristen Davis has been teaching Yoga and Alexander Technique since 1995. She is also a committed practitioner of Soto Zen Buddhism, having received the precepts in 2017. She is a member of the Brooklyn Zen Center sangha. For 14 years, she was the owner and director of Yogasana Center in Brooklyn, NY. Beyond her original 1600-hour Alexander Technique training, she completed a post-graduate Alexander training in the Art of Breathing with Jessica Wolf in 2006. Her yoga studies have been predominantly in the Iyengar tradition, most importantly with her yoga teacher and mentor, Donald Moyer.
