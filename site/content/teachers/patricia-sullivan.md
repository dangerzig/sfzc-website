Patricia’s instruction arises from over 45 years of teaching, and includes many streams of study. Her exploration of yoga began in 1971, and she studied in India with the Iyengars on three occasions in the 1980s. She taught Advanced Studies (teachers-in-training) throughout the 1990s and early 2000s before embarking on her own independent studies. She continues to offer retreats at Tassajara Zen Mountain Center, Green Gulch Farm and Zen Temple, and other venues. Patricia’s approach draws from a broad study of yogic traditions, Buddhist studies and practice, and her artwork. Qigong practice has also informed her teaching as has the study of postural analysis and re-education via the Egoscue method.
