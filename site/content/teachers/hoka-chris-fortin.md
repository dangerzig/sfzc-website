Chris is a Zen priest and teacher who received Dharma Transmission from Zoketsu Norman Fischer. She began practicing Buddhism at San Francisco Zen Center in 1976. She later became a licensed MFT psychotherapist and integrated spirituality and psychotherapy in her private practice for many years.

Chris founded and leads [Dharma Heart Zen](http://www.dharmaheartzen.com/) in Sonoma County and is a senior teacher in the [Everyday Zen Foundation](http://www.everydayzen.org/). She is active in anti-racism work. She leads Jizo Grief Ceremonies for Those Who Have Died, as well as trainings and Jizo Practice Groups. Chris cofounded [Veteran's PATH](http://www.honoringthepath.org/) to bring mindfulness and meditation practices to the wounds and trauma of war, and is currently working with wildland firefighters to help them meet the ever-increasing stressors of climate catastrophe.
