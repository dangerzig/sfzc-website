Dr. Liping Zhu, DAOM L.Ac., a licensed acupuncturist and the founder of Qi Dragon Healing Center, is a 13th generation Lineage Holder of the Tai Yi Swimming Dragon Sect. She also received her Qigong Master Level training in China in the Heavenly Essence Qigong lineage. She had 10 years residential training at San Francisco Zen Center and was Shuso (Head Student) in 2010. Currently, she lives in Humboldt County where she practices acupuncture and teaches Qigong and Taichi.

For more information, refer to Liping's [web site](http://www.qidragon.com/).
