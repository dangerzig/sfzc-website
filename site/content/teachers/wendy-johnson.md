Wendy Johnson lived and trained at Green Gulch Farm from 1975-2000. She is one of the founders of the Organic Farm and Garden Program at Green Gulch, a Lay Entrusted Teacher, and the author of [*Gardening at the Dragon’s Gate*](https://store.sfzc.org/product/gardening-at-the-dragon-s-gate-johnson-wendy-/189) (Bantam, 2008). Wendy is a garden mentor to the Edible Schoolyard program and a College of Marin instructor in the Environmental Landscape program at the Indian Valley Organic Farm and Garden project.
