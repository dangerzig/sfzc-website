[Green Gulch Farm](/locations/green-gulch-farm)

Date / Time

June 6, 2026 / 9:00am - 12:00pm

Event Type

Sitting Meditation & Sesshins

Description

A gentle introduction for beginning sitters. The morning will include instruction in sitting meditation (zazen) and walking meditation (kinhin), followed by alternating brief periods of zazen (20–25 minutes) and kinhin (10–15 minutes), and discussion with the leader.
