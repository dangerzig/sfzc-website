Tuesday evenings, 7:15 – 8:45 pm

Young Urban Zen (YUZ) is a group under the auspices of the San Francisco Zen Center, with a particular focus on those between 20 and 40 though anyone is welcome to attend.

It meets on Tuesday evenings for meditation and discussion about Zen practice. People of all experience levels are welcome. Those who have not meditated before may find it helpful to view a few of [SF Zen Center's meditation instruction videos](https://app.sfzc.org/p/qpZQYYjF), though this is not at all necessary before coming to Young Urban Zen.

Beyond Tuesday nights, members of the group regularly meet for social activities, volunteering, book club study, outings, and occasional retreats.

Young Urban Zen offers an opportunity to build meaningful community with people who care about practice and mindfulness.
