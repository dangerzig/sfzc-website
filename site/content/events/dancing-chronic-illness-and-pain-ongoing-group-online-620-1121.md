*Monthly on third Saturdays, 11 am–12:30 pm PT*  
June 20, July 18, August 15, September 19, October 17, and November 21

If you have previously participated in any of the “Dancing in the Dark Fields” series, we invite you to consider this ongoing monthly practice group, which will expand on those teachings and community.

Zenshin Florence Caplow, who will be teaching the class, has "danced" with auto-immune illness for the last two decades, including rheumatoid arthritis. She is also a Zen priest and teacher, co-editor of [*The Hidden Lamp: Stories from Twenty Five Centuries of Awakened Women*](https://store.sfzc.org/product/the-hidden-lamp-caplow-zenshin-florence-/19?cp=true&sa=false&sbp=false&q=true), and a Unitarian Universalist minister.

Zenshin Florence Caplow's book, [*Tend to Your Spirit: Mindful Living With Chronic Illness*](https://store.sfzc.org/product/tend-to-your-spirit-mindful-living-with-chronic-illness-by-julianne-lepp-and-florence-caplow-publ-10-7-25/R4WEWQ2KYC6HRMAQDUZFCZUO?cp=true&sa=false&sbp=false&q=true), is available to order from the SFZC online bookstore.
