Description

*Wednesday, May 6, 3 pm—Sunday, May 10, 2 pm*

This Introductory Four-Day Sesshin at Green Gulch Farm will focus on the basics of formal Zen practice, providing extended instruction in zazen, walking meditation, bowing, and chanting. It offers an introduction to intensive sesshin retreat practice for those newer to the Zen forms, and a chance for more experienced sitters to renew the fundamentals of formal practice.

Please review the example [sesshin schedule](https://www.sfzc.org/files/GGF-Intro-3-day-sesshin). The sesshin will consist of sitting and walking meditation, Dharma talks, and ceremonies, with light instruction as needed throughout. Meals will be eaten silently.

There will be an orientation on Wednesday at 3 pm. The sesshin will begin on Wednesday after dinner and conclude with lunch on Sunday.

This sesshin is open to anyone who has participated in an Introductory Half-Day Sitting at Green Gulch (or has comparable formal Zen experience) and previous participation in a One-Day Sitting at Green Gulch Farm is strongly recommended. If you have questions about your participation in this sitting, please contact the Ino, Head of the Meditation Hall, at [ggfino@sfzc.org](mailto:ggfino@sfzc.org).

To register for this sesshin, begin by completing [this form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/18OhoFISn-nJRPwnGb-yoNOIQ_QfRkvpWYghhfZEzkAk/edit?ts=67c5ec12).

Once you have been approved for the sesshin, you will be sent an email that will include a link through which you can process your payment and complete your registration.
