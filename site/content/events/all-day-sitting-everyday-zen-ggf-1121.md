Sitting Meditation & Sesshins

Description

Teachers: [Zoketsu Norman Fischer](/teachers/zoketsu-norman-fischer) and Senior [Everyday Zen](https://everydayzen.org/) Dharma teachers.

These traditional yet relaxed all-day sittings begin at 9:00 am and include meditation, a silent lunch, Dharma talk/discussion, and dokusan (private meeting with Norman or other teachers).

If you are new to meditation and Zen forms, please review one or two of the introductory practice instruction videos on this [SF Zen Center page](https://app.sfzc.org/p/qpZQYYjF).

Please arrive 15 minutes early (by 8:45 am) to check in.

\*Pre-registration is required, drop-ins cannot be accommodated.
