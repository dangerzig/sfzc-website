Description

Yoga and Zen are ancient traditions that cultivate our innate ability to embody the moment with presence, compassion, and fearlessness. In this retreat, we’ll explore the modern benefits of these timeless teachings that can invigorate and inspire us, and infuse wonder into our daily routines.

Our mornings begin with vigorous asana and end with a Dharma talk and meditation. We'll close our days together with Yin and Nidra practices to integrate the teachings and insights. Come join this brief retreat to revitalize your heart-mind and body with us!
