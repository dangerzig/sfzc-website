Description

Weekly meeting: Saturdays, 8:50 am – 12:30 pm

[Saturday Sangha](/practice-centers/city-center/zen-meditation-practice-city-center/saturday-sangha) is for anyone who joins the zazen schedule during the weekdays and/or on Saturday. Within the container of traditional forms, members of this group participate in doanryo training, temple care and kitchen practice, and study.

During the week, participation includes zazen with the invitation to join the doanryo. On Saturdays, the Saturday Sangha group bows in together in the courtyard at 8:50 am. The Saturday schedule typically ends at 12:30 pm, and includes zazen, a Dharma talk, tea and cookies, and study with a teacher.
