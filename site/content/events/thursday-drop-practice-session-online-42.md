Description

Every Thursday, 5:30 – 6:30 pm PT.

Join San Francisco Zen Center teachers for a weekly online drop-in session, offering opportunities to connect in real time.

Each session includes:

* 20 minutes of meditation
* A brief Dharma reflection
* Group discussion

These sessions provide a welcoming space for practice and community connection, open to practitioners of all levels.

For more information, visit the [Thursday Drop-in Practice Session homepage](https://www.sfzc.org/thursday-online-practice-session).
