Description

Join us in the beautiful Tassajara Valley, where we will enter a rhythm of practice that nourishes body, mind, and spirit. Guided by Lucy, this retreat draws inspiration from the timeless wisdom of Zen and Daoist traditions, inviting participants to explore what it means to bring life to life.

Our mornings will include Qigong practice adapted from the Eight Brocades, sitting and walking meditation, Zen stories and teachings, and group reflection. Afternoons offer gentle, restorative stretching that supports deep rest and ease. Between sessions, there will be spacious time to explore Tassajara’s trails, creek, and hot springs, allowing the body to settle and the mind to soften.

This retreat is an invitation to rediscover the fullness of being alive through presence and renewal.
