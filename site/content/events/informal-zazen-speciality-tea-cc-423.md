Description

We will gather weekly on Thursday evenings for Informal Zazen in the Buddha Hall at 300 Page Street. We will be exploring our zazen practice and how it is informed by other Zen practices. This will be followed by discussion and specialty teas in the Welcome Center.

Specialty teas are high-quality, often rare, loose-leaf teas that are distinguished by their unique flavors, processing methods, and origins. Suitable for beginners as well as experienced practitioners.
