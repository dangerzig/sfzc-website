Description

Please join in this decades-long tradition of Arbor Day at Green Gulch Farm as the community expresses our gratitude for the blessings of the earth. Following the 10:00 am Dharma talk, participants are invited to a complimentary lunch at 12:30 pm (optional).

At 1:30 pm, the Arbor Day event opens with words and a dedication ceremony. (Please follow the signs for directions to our opening gathering place.) Activities will include a ceremonial tree planting, weeding, sheet mulching, and creating seed balls. At 4:30 pm we will gather on the patio for tea, treats, and closing.

Feel free to bring your own gloves and basic gardening tools (clippers etc) if you have them.
