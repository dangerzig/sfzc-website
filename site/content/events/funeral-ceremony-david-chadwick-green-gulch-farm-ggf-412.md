Description

*Sunday, April 12, 3 – 6 pm, Green Gulch Farm*

Green Gulch Farm will host a funeral ceremony for David Chadwick on Sunday, April 12 at 3:00 pm. David Chadwick was a Soto Zen priest, author, archivist, and one of the earliest disciples of Shunryu Suzuki. He died on February 23, 2026 in Bali, Indonesia, where he had lived for the past thirteen years with his wife, Katrinka McKay.

The ceremony will take place in the zendo and will be followed by tea on the pool deck. Because space is limited, please register if you plan to attend the ceremony in-person. Parking will be extremely limited, so please plan to carpool.

For those who cannot attend in-person, the ceremony will also be live-streamed. The Zoom link is provided below.

If you would like to share memories or words of appreciation for David, you may do so on the [Remembering David Reich Chadwick blog post](https://blogs.sfzc.org/blog/2026/02/25/remembering-david-reich-chadwick/).
