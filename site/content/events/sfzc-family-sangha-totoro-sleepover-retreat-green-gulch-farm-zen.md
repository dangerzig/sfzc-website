Description

With Meikyo Chelsea True, Junsen Shelly Hughes, Kanjin Yael Raff Peskin, Kanki Elis Herman, and Samara Alima

Green Gulch Farm Zen Center is happy to welcome families to this annual two-night retreat. In this special offering, we explore family practice as a path for healing our ancestral lineages, discover the wisdom of children, and share the Dharma through connection with nature.

We are delighted to share that our 2026 retreat begins with a viewing of the beloved Japanese animated film, My Neighbor Totoro. Set in a tranquil countryside where forest spirits dwell, the film shines a light on the joy found in everyday experiences.

Throughout this retreat, families have time to practice together and we also offer dedicated time for parents and caregivers to receive support from our retreat team while children engage in separate activities.

Content is most appropriate for children ages 5-12.
