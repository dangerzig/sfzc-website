01/31/2026, Doshin Mako Voelkel, dharma talk at City Center. Abiding Abbot Dōshin Mako Voelkel discusses her January 2026 visit to Minneapolis with MARCH (Multifaith Antiracism, Change & Healing). In times of fear and violence, how does our practice teach us to pay attention to the body and access an appropriate response from a ground of presence and equanimity?
