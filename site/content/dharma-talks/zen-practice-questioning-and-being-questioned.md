09/27/2020, Tenshin Reb Anderson, dharma talk at Green Gulch Farm  
  
Tenshin Reb Anderson shares Zen stories as he demonstrates and teaches that Zen practice is questioning and being questioned. Ancient Zen ancestors throughout the past have found a way to meet one another through inquiry. Face to face, they saw buddha-nature reflected in one another. Following their path, we can do the same in today's crises. Mutuality is the whole of the way.
