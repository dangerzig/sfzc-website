In this talk Zoketsu Roshi brings up passages from Dogen's text Shobogenzo Zuimonki about the difference between lay practice and monastic practice, and, especially, about faith in karma -- that to do good unselfishly is the only way to live.  
03/07/2021, Zoketsu Norman Fischer, dharma talk at Green Gulch Farm.
