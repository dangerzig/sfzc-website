07/24/2021

In this talk, Senior Dharma Teacher Eijun Linda Ruth Cutts brings forth the spirit of the Mahayana Buddhist Vimalakirti Sutra, and the second turning of the wheel teachings. She suggests that the reconciliation of the truth of emptiness, the inconceivable which can not be grasped, can be found in our practice of zazen.  
07/24/2021, Eijun Linda Cutts, dharma talk at City Center.
