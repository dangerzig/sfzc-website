03/17/2024, Jiryu Rutschman-Byler, dharma talk at Green Gulch Farm.  
In this talk, Jiryu reflects on the disconnect that we often feel between a sense that in daily life we are “stuck in weeds,” and a faith or knowledge that we are “fundamentally satisfied and whole,” and points to Zen teachings on “welcoming what is here” as the way back to wholeness.
