Dharma talks are broadcast live from the Buddha Hall at City Center on Saturday mornings and Wednesday evenings. Additionally, Sunday morning Dharma talks are broadcast from Green Gulch Farm. Upcoming Dharma talks and select special events are listed below.

Recorded talks and select events are available for viewing on-demand in the [**Livestream Archive**](#archive) further below on this page. Recorded Dharma talks are also available later, in higher quality audio and video files, at the [**Dharma Talks Archives**](/offerings/dharma-talk-archive)page and now on the new [**SFZC Dharma App**](https://www.sfzc.org/app), as well as our [**YouTube channel**](https://www.youtube.com/@SFZenCenter)**.**
