03/27/2021

Ryushin Paul Haller invites us into deep inquiry of the the Bodhisattva vows. What is it to attend to and be of service to these vows in one's life? What is it to cross over to "only us," innumerably diverse? When us becomes more relevent than me. Being of service to Interbeing. How is this the full expression of my spirtual being? Everything is waiting for you.  
03/27/2021, Ryushin Paul Haller, dharma talk at City Center.
